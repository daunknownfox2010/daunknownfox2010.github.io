About

Half-Life 2 Campaign is a gamemode for Garry's Mod. The gamemode allows you to play Half-Life 2 in Garry's Mod without being given all the weapons and Sandbox equipment. Oh... and you can play with friends too!

Originally created by AMT, the premise is to create a vanilla Half-Life 2 experience within Garry's Mod without the need of a mod like Synergy. Garry's Mod is customisable too, allowing for optional content such as custom player models and more.

FAQs

Does this actually play Half-Life 2?

Short answer: Yes! This gamemode plays through Half-Life 2 in both singleplayer and multiplayer.

Does this support the Episodes?

Short answer: No. There is no proper support for the Episodes in Garry's Mod due to internal functions within Garry's Mod not actually supporting the full Episodic functions.

Does this include Half-Life 2 maps

HECK NO! That is very illegal and I could get in trouble for doing that. Please buy Half-Life 2 on Steam, it's fairly cheap these days.

Why are some scenes broken/skipped?

These scene errors are usually caused by Garry's Mod either not implementing necessary functions or the fact it's playing in multiplayer.

Can this play custom co-op maps?

Oh yes it can! There's a GitHub repository hosting some map lua files for a few custom co-op maps.

But... We already have Synergy/Obsidian Conflict!

Yes, I know. This is just an alternative, cause more options are better right?

You beta test Obsidian Conflict, why bother?

Read above. Once Obsidian Conflict does release on Steam though, I will put development of this gamemode behind.
